About Me

Heather Stevens is a Chicago based developer. With over a year as a community manager for a software start-up, Heather's interest moved towards the backend of software development. This interest led her to Northwestern's coding bootcamp, through which she learned HTML, CSS, and Javascript and produced multiple websites, debugged existing code, and worked in collaboration with other developers to build fully functional websites.

When she's not coding, Heather can be found training for her next marathon or spending time with her dog, Olive.
